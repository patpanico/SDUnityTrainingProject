The laboratory is a commonplace setting for learning through experimentation, whether in coursework, academic research or industry. In a laboratory, individuals refine techniques and procedures, reinforce theoretical concepts, and discover novel results. One of the primary concerns of any laboratory is safety, and each laboratory has their own approach to signifying harmful materials, protective equipment, and safety procedures. Especially with the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic, in-person course lab experiments require further precautionary measures. That's why the topic of our Senior Design Project was to simulate a lab environment.

Lab Training SDP by Patricio Panico, Keegan Yao, Soumya Potu, and Sohum Patel.
